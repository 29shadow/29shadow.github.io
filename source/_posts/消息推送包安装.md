### 华为推送安装

```html
<dependency>
    <groupId>com.huawei.pushplatform</groupId>
    <artifactId>push.java.sample</artifactId>
    <version>1.0</version>
</dependency>
```

```shell
$ mvn install:install-file -Dfile=D:\bme\jar\push.java.sample-1.0.jar -DgroupId=com.huawei.pushplatform -DartifactId=push.java.sample -Dversion=1.0 -Dpackaging=jar
```
